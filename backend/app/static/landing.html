Explainability

The Dreaming loop writes its own runbook.

During consolidation, Qwen3.7-Max reflects over raw episodes and distils procedural rules — readable, inspectable, with rationales it wrote itself. These are real rules from the live run:

IF text mentions 'Project Falcon' THEN category=technical (Project Falcon hardware, workspaces, and add-ons are technical)
IF text mentions requesting or processing a refund THEN category=account (refund requests are handled under account management)
IF text contains 'Acme' OR 'warehouse feed' THEN category=shipping (Acme and warehouse integrations are tied to shipping logistics)

Every decision in the console cites which of these rules fired — provenance down to the individual memory.

How it works

Modeled on the memory you already have.

Four tiers, one loop: experience is written as episodes; a background Dreaming pass distils what endures; decisions retrieve a few sharp rules instead of a pile of raw history.

01 · Experience

Episodic memory

Every routed ticket and its outcome is written as a raw, timestamped episode — the source of truth, indexed in pgvector.

02 · Consolidate

The Dreaming loop

On a schedule (Function Compute), Qwen3.7-Max reflects over history: distils rules and facts, credit-assigns, resolves conflicts, lets the unused decay.

03 · Decide

Budgeted retrieval

New tickets are answered with a finite context budget — a few high-confidence rules beat a long transcript, and every decision shows its evidence.
